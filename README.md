<div style="text-align:center"><h3>ABOUT THE BOOK</h3></div>

<h4>Author</h4>

<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wendell_Berge">Wendell Berge</a> (1903-1955).</p>

<h4>Publication date</h4>

<p>1946.</p>

<h4>Synopsis</h4>

<p>Berge was the head of United States <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Justice_Antitrust_Division">Antitrust enforcement</a> for many years. In this work, he details the danger of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cartel">economic cartels</a> to a free market economy.</p>
